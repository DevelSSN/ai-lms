\chapter{Literature Survey}
Education has significantly evolved in the years of past, from physical attendance to Massive Online Open Courses(MOOC). With online platforms being increasingly used to learn new concepts, it has become a new manifestation of the widely recognised "Two Sigma Problem", identified by  \cite{bloom19842}, with traditional platforms featuring classroom style videos, and are not tailored to students. With the adoption of AI into the field, it has now become possible to achieve any of the parameters set by \textcite{bloom19842}. In the current state of affairs, Large Language Models(LLMs) are being used to simulate intelligent conversations with the learner. We observe some limitations in the approaches taken, and proceed to highlight them in the following literature survey.
